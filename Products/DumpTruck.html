Dump Truck

A dump truck is a specialized heavy-duty vehicle designed for transporting and unloading loose materials, such as sand, gravel, soil, rocks, or construction debris. It features a large, open-top cargo bed, which can be tilted or raised at the rear to dump its contents. Dump trucks come in various sizes and configurations, including standard dump trucks with a single rear axle and larger models with multiple axles for increased carrying capacity. They are commonly used in construction, mining, agriculture, and waste management industries for hauling and depositing materials at construction sites, quarries, and disposal areas. Dump trucks play a vital role in the efficient transportation and distribution of bulk materials in various industries.
