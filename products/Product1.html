Road Roller

A road roller is a heavy construction vehicle equipped with either smooth or padfoot drums, designed to compact and flatten surfaces, commonly used in road construction, asphalt paving, and soil compaction projects. Operated from a cab on top, these machines use their weight and compaction mechanisms to remove air voids, enhancing surface stability and durability. Road rollers come in various types, including smooth drum, padfoot, tandem, and pneumatic rollers, each tailored to specific soil or asphalt compaction needs. Safety features like seat belts and rollover protection structures ensure operator safety, while regular maintenance is essential for optimal performance.
